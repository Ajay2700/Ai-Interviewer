Visual signals (useFaceTracking):
- MediaPipe FaceMesh detects landmarks and iris; computes EAR for blinks and simple gaze direction; detects head turns and low brightness
- Raises warnings with cooldown; tracks violations and termination threshold

Behavioral signals (useProctoring):
- Detects tab switching, window blur, right-click, copy/paste/cut, devtools-like shortcuts; best-effort multi-display hint
- Emits structured events; accumulates violations; provides environment readiness checks (mic, camera, single face, lighting)

Limitations:
- Browser sandbox prevents true system extension or tool enumeration; only detectable signals are captured and stored as JSONL on the backend
