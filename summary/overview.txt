AI Interviewer: a production-focused AI interviewing platform for technical assessments using voice. The system serves curated database questions and AI-generated questions, transcribes audio answers with Whisper, evaluates responses with a rubric-driven GPT model, tracks usage and limits, enforces basic proctoring, and provides an admin dashboard to manage the question bank.

Core flow:
1) Candidate starts interview (role, difficulty, mode)
2) Backend returns first question (DB for company/hybrid, GPT for AI-only or fallback)
3) Candidate records voice answer and uploads
4) Backend transcribes with Whisper and evaluates with GPT (score, confidence, strengths, weaknesses, improvements, verdict, feedback)
5) Next question comes from DB or GPT follow-up depending on mode and availability
6) Usage counters, cooldowns, rate limits, and per-session caps govern costs and fairness
