Question strategy:
- Company mode: DB-only questions for consistency and anti-hallucination
- AI mode: GPT-generated questions tailored by role and difficulty
- Hybrid mode: first N DB questions then GPT follow-ups based on prior answers

Evaluation:
- Strict rubric prompt returns JSON: score (0-10), confidence (0-100), strengths, weaknesses, improvements, verdict, feedback
- Parser tolerates formatting noise (backticks, extra text) and clamps values into ranges
- Empty/irrelevant answers forced to score 0 and fail verdict

Cost controls:
- Daily token and question quotas per user; per-requester minute-level rate limiting
- Per-session cooldown between next-question requests; per-interview caps on total and AI-only
- Short-lived in-memory cache for generated questions to reduce repeated cost
